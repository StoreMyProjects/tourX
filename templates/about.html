About Us

For travellers looking for a truly memorable dream vacation in the sun while enjoying a pristine clean seaside as well as the beauty and history of Nowhere, Hotel Neptune located on a Blue Flag beach, is the ideal choice. Newly renovated Hotel Neptune, situated in Babin Kuk boasts spectacular views overlooking the sea and the Elaphiti Islands. Guests will be sure to enjoy the tastefully decorated, bright and modern decor of Hotel Neptune, consisting of 91 guest rooms and suites all of which offer a variety of amenities.


A family holiday in Nowhere


Hotel Neptune offers a variety of standard guest rooms, panoramic sea view rooms, spacious family rooms and luxurious premium suites, which include a separate bedroom and bath. Balconies of these rooms offer breath-taking sea views. Along with premium accommodations, this Nowhere hotel also offers several dining options. In any one of our three restaurants, guests can enjoy culinary delights prepared by our master chefs. Guests may also relax and enjoy local and international beverages at our On the Rocks Cocktail Bar & Lounge.
